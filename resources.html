ScribeAmerica

In the medical field and don't know how to gain experience? Check out ScribeAmerica! ScribeAmerica is a foundation that offers training to individuals who are aspiring to be a part of the medical field. If accepted into the program, you will work as a medical scribe, who closely works with a physician by performing documentation in the EHR, gathering information for the patient's visit, and partnering with the physician to deliver the pinnacle of efficient patient care.

Remote Woman

Are you a woman aspiring to be a part of the evergrowing tech community? Check out Remote Woman, a website that provides women with remote jobs at female-friendly companies. RW has connected thousands of women to incredible jobs that allow them to work in the comfort of their own home.

Meetup

The horrors of having to network is something many of us have battled with. At Meetup.com, networking has never been easier and more relaxing. At meetup, you have the oppertunity to virtually chat with groups all over the world and get your name and resume out there. Simply search a sector you are interested in (try checking out women in STEM groups!) and RSVP to any upcoming events!

Levels.Fyi

The gender pay gap or gender wage gap is the average difference between the remuneration for men and women who are working. Currently, the adjusted pay gap stands at 16-cents among all workers in 2020. Studies have shown that women tend to shy away from negotiating their salaries. With that, a lot of job descriptions do not disclose their pay! Levels.fyi is a website dedicated to salary transparency. Simply search the company name, and get an inside look on the different roles offered and what exactly the salary is, along with the total breakdown (salary, stock, and bonus).
