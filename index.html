Work With Women Who Get It

Women In STEM at SJU is committed to providing only the best resources for our members. We have brought in several women in several different STEM sectors to speak on their experiences in the field. With that, they provided valuable information on how to tackle female-focused issues on the field, and serve as incredibly motivating figures to us.

Giving Back

We want our impact to go past the walls of St. John's. UNICEF reports that 129 million girls do not have access to education, and only 49 percent of countries have achieved gender parity in primary education. WIS is determined to do everything in our abilities to provide these women with educational resources through charity events.

Networking and Community

What better place to find like-minded women than in Women In STEM SJU? Surround yourself with a community of women who have similar career goals, whilst exposing yourself to different STEM pathways. Bounce off ideas with one another, collaborate on projects, work on homework together, and so much more.

Advance Your Career

Women In STEM will be holding several events throughout the semester to help out as much as possible in advancing your career.
